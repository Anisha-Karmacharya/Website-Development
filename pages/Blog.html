THE EFFECT OF INTERNET ON BUSINESS

This blog pinpoint the advantages and disadvantages of using internet technology in business.
AUGUST 21, 2019

Two decades from now, starting a business meant looking for a place in a area with good reach for public. All that changed with the inception of the Internet. A business is no longer dependent on its local customer base for its survival; it now has a worldwide audience for its goods and services. The Internet has changed not only a business' customer base, but how a business communicates with its employees, and finds and manages the competition.Although it can now seem like the internet is the be-all and end-all of business activities, it is always best to take into account the needs of ones business and to weigh the advantages and disadvantages of including the internet in business model.

Obviously, internet has been impacting businesses, employment, environment, entrepreneurship, societies, education, and politics. But it has both positive and negative impacts. Marketing opportunities are high while big brands can have hands on marketing.Small scale businesses are unable to compete with big brands and their digital marketing methods.The impact of internet on market competition is high. The businesses using digital marketing, cloud computing, online automation tools, online accounting, eCommerce and artificial intelligence application and getting more benefits than competitors. But for those who cannot change as per the technologies and trends it is a burden.

And when every technology and tool is connected with the internet then it will impact the traditional and ongoing business models negatively and positively. The simple solutions are that business will needs adaptivity for the change according to the time else internet can impact the growth negatively.When business is adaptive to change as per the internet technologies then they will see growth in sales and net profit. And if they are unable to change they will see a decrease in demands, sales, and profit. and even it’s possible that many have to close the business due to lack of innovation.That’s how internet impacts the success and failure of the business. In one line, the Internet is the main part of the business. and no one can ignore it in life, business, education.

The internet is a powerful tool not only for businesses but also for consumers. Handle it with care and in accordance with the needs and model of your business, so that the advantages are maximized and that the disadvantages can be mitigated.

Online Shopping Habit

This blog is about the change internet has bought on our day to day life .
AUGUST 15, 2019

Nowadays in new era of technology, internet has already become part of our life. As Internet is becoming more widely used, we almost can find or do everything via internet. As many businesses are attempting to globalize their sales, Internet becomes the primary means of advertising and selling their products or services worldwide. Many businesses are creating web pages in addition to their retail stores, and many start their businesses with online stores alone

The implications of online shopping website toward the society have advantages and disadvantages. It depends on how people use on it. If people use it in right way, it will bring a lot of positive benefit. Kimberly Palmer (2007) said, according to Forrester Research, more than half of US households regularly shop on the web, but online purchases still make up only 7 percent of total retail sales. Nowadays, consumers can buy everything over the web, which mean it help us save time and money. Convenient is the main advantage for online shopping website, because it allows consumer shop at anywhere and anytime with just only a personal computer or laptop with an internet access. It also save consumer traveling time to retail stores. Convenience includes the overall ease of finding a product, time spent on shopping, post purchase service, complete contact information, and minimization of overall shopping effort.
